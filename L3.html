History

Hallstatt’s history dates back over 7,000 years. It became wealthy through ancient salt mining, shaping one of Europe’s oldest Alpine settlements.

Culture

Hallstatt reflects traditional Austrian Alpine life, wooden houses, lake views and strong heritage traditions.

Location

Located in Upper Austria between a serene lake and the dramatic Dachstein Alps.

Why it Matters

Hallstatt blends natural beauty with deep European history, giving its name to the ancient Hallstatt Culture.
